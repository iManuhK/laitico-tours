About Laitico Tours & Travel

At Laitico Tours & Travel, we believe that travel is more than just visiting places; it's about creating unforgettable experiences and memories that last a lifetime. As a premier tours and travel company, we are dedicated to providing our clients with unique journeys tailored to their dreams and interests.

Our team can implement and monitor your travel policies, as well as keep your business informed on travel industry issues through regular communication and meetings.
For the individual traveler, we take the stress out of planning a vacation, so you can start relaxing before your depart. Whether you travel locally or internationally, we provide information on your destination, so you know what to expect and where to find the services you need.

Who We Are

Founded with a passion for exploration and a deep appreciation for nature, we are a team of travel enthusiasts committed to showcasing the beauty and diversity of our world. From the breathtaking savannahs of Maasai Mara to the urban wildlife wonder of Nairobi National Park, we specialize in crafting adventures that inspire and connect.

What We Do

  • Customized Tour Packages: Designed to suit your budget and preferences.
  • Safari Adventures: Discover the magic of African wildlife.
  • Cultural Experiences: Immerse yourself in the rich traditions and history of our destinations.
  • Travel Planning & Support: From itinerary creation to on-ground assistance, we’ve got you covered.

Why Choose Us?

  • Local Expertise: Our team comprises seasoned guides and travel planners with extensive knowledge of each destination.
  • Personalized Service: Every trip is uniquely crafted to meet your expectations.
  • Commitment to Sustainability: We support eco-friendly tourism to preserve the natural beauty of our planet.
  • Unmatched Experiences: We go beyond the ordinary to deliver extraordinary moments.

Our Mission

To inspire, connect, and transform lives through meaningful travel experiences while promoting sustainable tourism practices.

Our Vision

To be the leading travel company, known for exceptional service and unforgettable adventures.

Let us take you on a journey of discovery and wonder. Whether it’s your first safari, a family vacation, or a solo escape, Laitico Tours & Travel is here to make your dream journey a reality.
